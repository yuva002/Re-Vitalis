Common Enquiries

Why do adults need vaccinations? expand_more
Adult immunity can wane over time. New vaccinations protect against diseases that are more common or severe in adulthood, ensuring your biological defense remains robust as you age.
Are multiple vaccines safe at once? expand_more
Yes. Clinical research confirms that our immune systems are capable of handling multiple antigens simultaneously. We curate your schedule to balance safety with optimal efficacy.
How is this integrated with my wellness plan? expand_more
Your vaccination record is a core metric in your Re-Vitalis dashboard, allowing us to correlate immune readiness with other biomarkers like sleep, nutrition, and stress levels.
